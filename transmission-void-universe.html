There Is a Void in the Universe with Your Name on It

You are here to create something that only you can create. It has been knocking on the door of your soul for a very long time.

You already feel it. That pull — the one that doesn't quite go away no matter how busy you get, no matter how much you distract yourself, no matter how many times you decide it is not the right time yet. That persistent, inconvenient, beautiful knowing that you are here for something.

It has been knocking on the door of your soul. Quietly at first, then louder. And even when you have done everything in your power to ignore it — to stay small, to stay safe, to stay inside the version of yourself that feels manageable — it keeps coming back. Because it is not going anywhere. It was always meant to find you.

The Universe Left a Space That Only You Can Fill

There is a void in the universe with your name on it. Not a metaphor. A literal gap in the fabric of what exists — a contribution, a creation, a presence, a way of moving through the world that will never exist if you do not bring it into being.

No one else can do it. Not someone with a similar background, similar gifts, similar message. Not someone who is further along, more confident, more established. Not someone who has already figured out the parts you haven't figured out yet. Only you. With your specific pain and your specific perspective and your specific way of holding a room and your specific ability to see what others cannot.

There is something you are supposed to create that only you can create. The void has your name on it.

Your Doubt Is Not Special

Here is something that may sound harsh but is actually one of the most liberating things you can hear: your doubt is not unique. Your fear is not special. The voice that tells you you are not qualified, not ready, not enough — that voice speaks to everyone. Every single person who has ever created anything of significance has sat in that same uncertainty, felt that same freeze, told themselves that same story.

You are not special because you feel doubt and fear and judgment and shame. None of us are exempt from it. We all feel it.

But you are special in a way that matters far more: there is a void in the universe with your name on it. And that void does not go away just because you decide to wait a little longer.

The Excuses Are Familiar. The Void Remains.

Maybe your nervous system freezes at the thought of being fully seen. Maybe you worry about what people will think — your family, your old friends, the people who knew you before. Maybe you wonder if you have enough time, enough energy, enough credentials.

We already know all the excuses. We have rehearsed every single one. And they are all, in their own way, completely understandable — and completely beside the point. Because the void remains. The calling remains. The thing that only you can create is still waiting, still knocking, still patient in the way that only truly important things are patient.

Your potential is supposed to be actualised. Your dreams are meant to come true. And you — you are the one to do it.

Not after you heal more. Not when the conditions are right. Not when you have proof that it will work. Now. With everything you already are, exactly as you already are — which was always, already, more than enough.

With Love,
Madalena Dragon

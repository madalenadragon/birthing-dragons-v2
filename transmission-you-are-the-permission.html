You Are the Permission

Stop waiting. You are the one you have been waiting for.

There is a particular kind of waiting that highly sensitive, deeply aware women get trapped in. It doesn't look like laziness. It doesn't feel like giving up. It looks responsible. Humble, even. It looks like:

Waiting until you're ready. Waiting until someone believes in you. Waiting until the conditions are right, until you've done more inner work, until you have more proof that you are enough to take up the space you feel called to take up.

And underneath all of that waiting is one quiet, devastating belief: that the permission to become who you are meant to be lives somewhere outside of you.

It does not.

Who Told You That You Needed Permission?

Your family. Society. The industry. Other leaders who built their authority on being the ones who grant approval. The conditioning was subtle and it was everywhere, the message that there is a correct order to things, and that your job is to wait your turn, prove yourself first, be validated by something or someone before you are allowed to be fully, unapologetically yourself.

You don't need to be what your family wants you to be. You don't need to fit into someone else's version of spirituality, leadership, or success. You don't need the industry's stamp of approval. You don't need the internet to tell you that you're ready.

You don't need permission. You don't need proof. You don't need to wait for anyone to tell you you can.

You Are Allowed to Be Everything

Big. Loud. Soft. Powerful. Wild. Rich. Magnetic. Divine. Paradoxical. Contradictory. Still figuring it out and fully showing up at the same time.

You are allowed to want what you want. To charge what you want to charge. To say what you want to say. To build the thing that only you can build, in the way only you can build it. To be the version of you that you've been practising in private and calling impractical, too much, too soon.

There is no authority higher than the one you carry inside yourself. There never was.

You Are the Proof

You don't need to accumulate more evidence that you are worthy. You don't need more testimonials, more credentials, more years of experience. The fact that you have been called, that you feel the magnitude of what you are here to do, even when it terrifies you, that is already the proof.

Not proof that you are perfect. Proof that you are ready enough. That the readiness grows through the doing, not before it. That the woman you are becoming is available to you right now, not after you've healed more, achieved more, become more.

You are the proof. You are the permission. You are the one you've been waiting for.

Stop waiting. There is no other moment. This is it. And you, exactly as you are, right now, are more than enough to begin.

With Love,
Madalena Dragon

Your Body is Your First Oracle

You keep asking for signs. But have you been listening to what your body is already saying?

We live in a time of spiritual seeking. More people than ever are turning to tarot, astrology, human design, spirit guides, psychics, searching for clarity, for direction, for a sign that they are on the right path.

And all of this can be beautiful. Sacred, even. But somewhere along the way, we forgot to consult the most ancient, most accurate oracle we were ever given, the one that has been with us since before we were born.

Your body.

Your body is the first and most accurate Oracle. Before the Spirit Guides, the Tarot, the psychics, it is always communicating with you. But are you listening?

Your Body Has Been Whispering

Your body speaks in the only language it knows, sensation. And it has been trying to reach you for a long time.

That knot in your throat when you swallow your truth. The gut twist when you abandon your own knowing and follow someone else's advice. The exhaustion that descends after you betray your boundaries, even the small ones. The aliveness that floods your body when you say yes to something that lights your soul on fire.

These are not random sensations. They are messages. Precise, personal, and perfectly timed. Your body doesn't speak in vague metaphors or symbolic imagery that needs interpretation, it speaks directly, physically, undeniably.

Your Body Doesn't Lie

The mind is brilliant, and the mind is a master of deception. It can rationalise. Justify. Minimise. Convince you that what you felt wasn't real, that you were overreacting, that you should push through, that the opportunity is too good to let a feeling get in the way.

Your body cannot do any of that. It reveals what your mind hides from you and runs from. It keeps score of every suppressed truth, every swallowed word, every moment you abandoned your own knowing to keep the peace or look a certain way.

It holds the wisdom you keep outsourcing to the universe, to God, to spirit guides, to anyone outside yourself.

You Want Clear Guidance? Come Inward.

The greatest sign is not in a card pull. It is not in a synchronicity, a dream, or a number pattern, though all of those can be beautiful confirmations of what you already know.

The greatest sign is in you. It always has been.

Start listening to your body the way you would listen to God, to the universe, to the wisest teacher you have ever encountered. Because that is exactly what it is. The most direct line to your deepest truth, available to you every moment of every day, no reading required.

Listen to your body like you would listen to God. The greatest sign is not out there. It is in you. Are you listening?

The question was never whether the guidance was available. The question is whether you are willing to trust what you already hear.

With Love,
Madalena Dragon

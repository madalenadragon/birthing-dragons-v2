Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Birthing Dragons ("we", "us", or "our"), operated by Madalena Morbey, collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit birthingdragons.com.

1. Who We Are

Birthing Dragons is a spiritual mentorship and education business operated by Madalena Morbey. For any privacy-related enquiries, contact us at hello@birthingdragons.com.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information, name and email address when you sign up for our newsletter, free resources, or programmes.
  • Payment information, processed securely through our payment providers (Groove). We never store your card details.
  • Usage data, pages visited, time on site, browser type, via Google Analytics (anonymised).
  • Communications, messages you send us via email or contact forms.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver the programmes, courses, and resources you have purchased or signed up for.
  • To send you emails you have opted into (newsletters, programme updates).
  • To improve our website and understand how visitors use it.
  • To process payments and fulfil orders.
  • To respond to your enquiries.

4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, we process your data based on: your consent, the performance of a contract, or our legitimate interests in running and improving our business.

5. Cookies

We use cookies for website analytics (Google Analytics) and to remember your preferences. You can manage cookie preferences via the banner on your first visit, or through your browser settings.

6. Third Parties

We use trusted third-party services including Google Analytics, Groove (payments and CRM), and email delivery platforms. These providers have their own privacy policies and process data in accordance with GDPR where applicable.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, email us at hello@birthingdragons.com. You may also unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. Our website is served over HTTPS and we use reputable third-party providers with strong security practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will always be available at this URL with the revised date shown above.

11. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@birthingdragons.com.
